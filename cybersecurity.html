Defense in depth

Six layers, one team watching

No single tool keeps a business safe. Layers do — so if one slips, the next one holds. Here's where we stand guard.

Identity & logins

Strong sign-ins with multi-factor authentication on every account, and admin access locked down to the people who actually need it.

Devices

Every laptop and workstation protected, encrypted, and patched — so a lost machine or an old flaw isn't an open door.

Email

Filtering that catches phishing and fake invoices before your team ever sees them. The messages that get through are the real ones.

Network

Business-grade firewall and Wi-Fi, configured properly and kept current — not the box your internet provider dropped off.

Monitoring & response

Someone is watching 24/7. Suspicious activity gets spotted early and shut down before it can spread.

Updates & patching

The unglamorous work that closes most doors. Software stays current on a schedule, not when someone remembers.

Compliance & cyber insurance

Cyber insurance questionnaires and audits keep getting longer, and "we think we're covered" is not an answer anyone enjoys giving. We match your setup to what insurers and auditors actually require — then keep the evidence current as things change.

So when the renewal or the questionnaire lands on your desk, the answers are already written down. You forward it to us, and your week stays yours.

What you get

  • Your security matched to what your policy and auditors actually require
  • Evidence kept current year-round, not rebuilt in a panic each renewal
  • Insurance questionnaires answered with us, accurately, the first time
  • Plain-English answers when an auditor or a big customer asks how you handle security

Backup & Disaster Recovery

Everyone says they have backups. We test ours — by actually running the restore, not by trusting a green checkmark. Your data gets copied where ransomware can't encrypt it or quietly delete it, and we practice bringing it back before you ever need to.

There's a written plan for what comes back first, so a very bad day becomes a short one instead of a long week.

What you get

  • Copies ransomware can't encrypt or quietly delete
  • Restores proven by running them, not by a green checkmark
  • A written plan for what comes back first and how fast
  • Coverage for servers and laptops, plus the Microsoft 365 data most backup setups miss

Security awareness training

Most attacks start with one convincing email. That makes your team the first line of defense — and with a little practice, the strongest one. Short, regular training plus simulated phishing turns "easiest target" into "best layer you have."

No blame culture, ever. When people know they won't be shamed for clicking, they report the weird email instead of hiding it — and that early report is what stops an incident cold.

What you get

  • Short, regular training people actually finish
  • Simulated phishing that builds instinct without embarrassing anyone
  • A team that reports suspicious emails instead of hiding mistakes
  • Training records ready for the insurance questionnaire

Cameras & door access

Security isn't only digital. We install and maintain the cameras and badge readers protecting your building — on the same network we already run, so one team owns the wiring and the gear on the wall.

The right people get in, nobody else does, and you can check the footage from your phone wherever you are.

What you get

  • Cameras picked for your space, installed cleanly
  • Badge and keypad access for doors, managed for you
  • Footage you can pull up on your phone from anywhere
  • Maintenance included, so it all still works the day it matters

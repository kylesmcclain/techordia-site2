Fair questions

Things people ask us

Will I talk to a real person?

Yes. An engineer answers the phone — not a receptionist taking a message, not a chatbot. That's been true since 2010, and it's the part of the business we protect hardest.

Do you come onsite?

Yes. Most fixes are faster remotely, but when hardware needs hands we drive over. We serve the Greater San Francisco Bay Area from Alameda, so we're never far.

Do I have to be a Managed IT client?

Help desk support is part of our managed plan — unlimited support, one flat monthly fee, no lock-in. That's how we can answer fast without metering every call. If you have a one-off piece of work instead, take a look at IT Projects.

What about after hours?

Monitoring runs around the clock, so a lot gets caught and handled whether anyone's awake or not. For support itself, we're straight with clients about exactly what's covered after hours — and like everything else, it goes in writing.

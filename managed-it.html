What's inside

Everything the plan covers, spelled out

No mystery bundle, no add-on maze. Here's each piece of Managed IT, what it means in plain English, and what you actually get.

Help Desk & Support

Something breaks, you call, a person answers. Not a phone tree, not a chatbot: an engineer who already knows your office, your software, and probably your dog's name. Most problems get fixed remotely while you're still on the line, and when the fix needs hands on hardware, we drive over.

What you get

  • Real engineers on the phone, with response times we put in writing
  • Remote fixes for the everyday stuff, onsite visits when it's physical
  • New hires set up before day one, departing accounts locked the day they leave
  • We deal with your internet provider and printer company, so you never sit on hold

Cybersecurity & Compliance

We keep the bad guys out of your logins, email, and devices, and we keep the paperwork that proves it. So when the cyber-insurance renewal or an auditor comes asking, the answers are already written down instead of eating your week.

What you get

  • MFA, device protection, and email filtering, watched every day
  • Suspicious activity caught early and shut down fast
  • Your setup matched to what insurers and auditors actually ask for
  • Evidence kept current, so renewals stop being a scramble

Cloud & Microsoft 365

Microsoft 365 and Azure are great, right up until nobody owns them. We set yours up properly, keep them secure, and trim the licenses you're paying for but nobody's using. Still on an old server in a closet? We'll tell you honestly whether the cloud is worth it for you.

What you get

  • Email, Teams, SharePoint, and OneDrive that just work
  • Azure and the rest of your cloud watched and maintained
  • A license review that usually finds money hiding in plain sight
  • Moves to the cloud planned and handled start to finish

Backup & Disaster Recovery

Everyone says they have backups. We actually test ours. Your data gets copied somewhere ransomware can't reach, and we practice the restore before you ever need it, so a very bad day turns into a short one.

What you get

  • Backups ransomware can't encrypt or quietly delete
  • Restores proven by doing them, not by a green checkmark
  • A written recovery plan: what comes back first, and how fast
  • Coverage for servers, laptops, and your Microsoft 365 data

Cameras & Door Access

Security isn't only digital. We install and look after the cameras, badge readers, and door controls that protect the physical side of your business, all tied into the same network we already run. One team for the wires and the gear on the wall.

What you get

  • Cameras picked for your space, installed cleanly, and kept working
  • Badge and keypad door access, so the right people get in and nobody else
  • Footage you can pull up from your phone, wherever you are
  • Maintenance included, so it all still works the day you need it

IT Strategy & Consulting

Advice from people who don't earn a commission on the answer. We map out your next year or three, put honest numbers next to it, and clean up the pile of overlapping tools and licenses every growing company collects. What to buy, what to skip, what to stop paying for.

What you get

  • A technology roadmap and budget written in plain English
  • Quarterly sit-downs that keep the plan matched to the business
  • Vendor and license cleanup that usually pays for itself
  • Straight answers, even when the answer is "don't buy it"

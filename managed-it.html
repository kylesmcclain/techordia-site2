What's inside

Everything the plan covers, spelled out

Here's each piece of the plan and what it actually gets you. No add-ons hiding in the fine print.

Help Desk & Support

When something breaks, you call and a person answers. Not a phone tree and not a chatbot. The engineer on the line already knows your office and your setup, because we keep the same people on your account. Most fixes happen remotely while you're still on the phone. When the problem is physical, we drive over.

What you get

  • Real engineers on the phone, with response times we put in writing
  • Remote fixes for everyday problems, onsite visits when hardware needs hands
  • New hires set up and working before their first morning
  • We handle your internet provider and your other vendors, so you never wait on hold

Cybersecurity & Compliance

We keep criminals out of your accounts and your email, and we keep the paperwork that proves your defenses are real. When the cyber-insurance renewal lands or an auditor starts asking questions, the answers are already written down.

What you get

  • Strong logins, protected devices, filtered email, and someone actually watching
  • Suspicious activity caught early and shut down before it spreads
  • Your setup matched to what insurers and auditors actually require
  • Evidence kept current, so renewals stop costing you a week

Cloud & Microsoft 365

Microsoft 365 and Azure work well when somebody owns them. For our clients, that somebody is us. Almost every business we take on turns out to be paying for licenses nobody uses. We find them and cancel them.

What you get

  • Email, Teams, SharePoint, and OneDrive working the way they're supposed to
  • Your cloud accounts watched and maintained, not set up and forgotten
  • A license review that usually pays for part of the service
  • Server-to-cloud moves planned and handled end to end

Backup & Disaster Recovery

Everyone says they have backups. We actually test ours. Your data gets copied somewhere ransomware can't reach, and we practice the restore before you ever need it. A very bad day becomes a short one.

What you get

  • Backups that ransomware can't encrypt or quietly delete
  • Restores proven by actually running them, not by a green checkmark
  • A written plan for what comes back first and how fast
  • Coverage for your servers and laptops, plus the Microsoft 365 data most backup setups miss

Cameras & Door Access

Security isn't only digital. We install and maintain the cameras and door controls that protect your building, on the same network we already manage. One team owns the wiring and the gear on the wall.

What you get

  • Cameras picked for your space and installed cleanly
  • Badge and keypad access, so the right people get in and nobody else does
  • Footage you can pull up on your phone from anywhere
  • Maintenance included, so it all still works the day it matters

IT Strategy & Consulting

When you're deciding what to buy next, the advice shouldn't come from someone earning commission on the answer. We map out the next few years with honest numbers attached. Sometimes the right answer is to buy nothing, and we'll say so.

What you get

  • A technology roadmap and budget written in plain English
  • A quarterly sit-down to keep the plan matched to the business
  • Cleanup of the overlapping tools and licenses every growing company collects
  • Straight answers, even when they're not what you were hoping to hear

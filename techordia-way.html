Who we are

The Techordia Way

Techordia is built on a simple idea: take the qualities you'd want in a person and run a business on them. Here's where we came from and the standard we hold ourselves to.

Our story

Twelve years of picking up the phone

Techordia goes back to 2014. Two of us run it. One spent his career inside complicated systems, learning how to make them simple for the people who use them and how to take care of those people when something breaks. The other was shaped by the military, where the mission gets finished and the people beside you matter more than the task list.

Put the two together and you get how we operate. When something breaks at your office, we own it from the first alert to the final fix. We won't stall and we won't hide behind jargon. If we caused the problem, you'll hear it from us first.

Some of our clients have been with us for the better part of a decade. A few wrote about it on Yelp, where we hold five stars across eight reviews. That track record is the thing we protect hardest, and it's why we put our standard in writing and gave it a name: The Techordia Way.

Vision

The most trusted technology partner for ambitious businesses

We want to be the team businesses call first. When technology stops being a worry, your energy goes back into your customers and your people.

Mission

To take the entire weight of technology off the businesses we serve

We run every layer of your IT and we answer for all of it, from the help desk to the cloud. Decisions get explained in plain English, with real numbers behind them. Partnerships here are earned the old-fashioned way: we pick up the phone and we tell the truth. When technology just works, you're free to do your best work.

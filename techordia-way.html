Our story

Two of us, since 2010

Techordia started in 2010 with two owners and one rule: finish the job. One of us is the engineer — the one who takes complicated systems apart until they're simple, and looks after the people using them. The other came up through the military and runs the business the way he ran missions: it gets done, and the people next to you come first.

One builds it right. One makes sure it ships. When something breaks at your office, we own it from first alert to final fix — no stalling, no jargon. If we caused it, you hear it from us first.

Some clients have been with us nearly all of those fifteen years. One wrote on Yelp that they've “partnered with Techordia since 2010 and have never been disappointed.” Five stars, eight reviews. That record is what we protect hardest — so we wrote the standard down and gave it a name.

The Techordia Way.

Techordia was built on a simple idea: bring the qualities of a great person into how a business runs. Here's the story behind the name, and the standards we hold to every day.

Our story

Why we built Techordia.

Techordia was built around a clear idea: blend a deep passion for technology and caring for people with a disciplined, mission-driven mindset. One co-owner comes from a civilian background, focused on making complex systems simple, keeping environments stable, and relentlessly looking after clients and their members when problems hit. The other is a military-trained partner whose experience brings discipline, resilience, and a “finish the mission” mentality into how we operate every day.

That combination is what makes Techordia who we are today: a team that teaches, trains, fights for our clients when things go wrong, and fully owns the work we do from first alert to final resolution. When something breaks or the stakes are high, we don’t stall, point fingers, or hide in jargon. We move fast, communicate clearly, and take full ownership from start to finish. Our can-do attitude isn’t a slogan; it’s the standard for every ticket, every project, and every relationship. That is what we call The Techordia Way.

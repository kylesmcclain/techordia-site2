The delivery process

Five steps. No surprises on the big day

01 · SCOPE

Agree on the goal

We agree on what done looks like before any work starts.

02 · PREPARE

Find problems early

We build and test everything in advance, so problems show up in rehearsal instead of on your busiest Monday.

03 · CUTOVER

Make the switch

We schedule the switch and tell everyone what to expect. A way to undo it stays on standby the whole time.

04 · TEST

Prove it works

Real people try it before we call it finished. If it's not right, we keep going.

05 · HANDOFF

Hand it off clean

Everything gets documented and handed over working, with nothing left for you to chase.

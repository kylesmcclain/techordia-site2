The delivery process

Five steps, one calm cutover

01 · SCOPE

Agree on done

We define what finished looks like before any work starts.

02 · PREPARE

Rehearse it first

Everything gets built and tested in advance, so surprises show up in rehearsal — not on your busiest Monday.

03 · CUTOVER

Flip the switch

We schedule the change and tell everyone what to expect. The undo button stays on standby.

04 · TEST

Prove it works

Real people try it before we call it done. Not right? We keep going.

05 · HANDOFF

Hand it off clean

Documented, working, and nothing left for you to chase.

Then we keep it running

Most projects roll straight into Managed IT, so what we build stays looked after.

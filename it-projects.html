The delivery process

Five stages. No surprises on cutover day.

01 · SCOPE

Define success

Goals, constraints, inventory, and a fixed definition of "done" before work starts.

02 · PREPARE

De-risk the move

Build, stage, and pilot. We find the problems in a lab, not in production.

03 · CUTOVER

Execute the plan

A scheduled, communicated cutover with rollback ready if anything drifts.

04 · TEST

Prove it works

Validate against the success criteria with real users before we call it done.

05 · HANDOFF

Operationalize

Documentation, monitoring, and a clean handoff into managed operations.

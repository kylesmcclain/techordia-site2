The delivery process

Five steps. No surprises on the big day

01 · SCOPE

Agree on the goal

We agree on what "done" looks like before any work starts.

02 · PREPARE

Find problems early

We build and test everything in advance, so problems show up in rehearsal, not on your busiest Monday.

03 · CUTOVER

Make the switch

We schedule it, tell everyone what to expect, and keep a way to undo it on standby.

04 · TEST

Prove it works

Real people try it before we call it finished. If it's not right, we keep going.

05 · HANDOFF

Hand it off clean

Everything documented, monitored, and handed over working, with nothing left for you to chase.
